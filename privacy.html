Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026  ·  Effective: 1 April 2026

1. What We Collect

When you create an account or join the waitlist, we collect:

  • Account information — name, email address, company name, industry
  • Financial data you connect — bank transactions, invoices, or accounting records you explicitly link to the platform
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session duration (via anonymised analytics)
  • Device & technical data — browser type, IP address, operating system

We do not collect data from third parties without your explicit authorisation.

2. How We Use It

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the Prospera Analytics platform
  • Generate financial insights, forecasts, and risk assessments for your business
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, security alerts)
  • Send product updates and waitlist communications (you can unsubscribe at any time)

We will never use your financial data to train AI models without your explicit, separate consent.

3. Data Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards to protect your data:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • Strict access controls — only authorised personnel can access production data
  • Regular security reviews and penetration testing

For more details, see our Security & Trust page.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Full stop.

We may share data with trusted sub-processors (e.g. cloud infrastructure, analytics) under strict contractual terms. A list of current sub-processors is available on request.

5. Your Rights (PDPA)

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA 2012), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent at any time (this may limit platform functionality)
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@prospera.io. We will respond within 30 days.

6. PDPA Compliance

Data Protection Officer (DPO)
Prospera Analytics has appointed a Data Protection Officer in accordance with the PDPA. Contact: dpo@prospera.io

We are registered with the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) of Singapore. Our data retention policy ensures personal data is not kept longer than necessary for the stated purpose.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only (session management, security). We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some platform features may not function correctly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify registered users by email of any material changes to this Privacy Policy at least 14 days before they take effect.

9. Contact Us

  • Privacy enquiries: privacy@prospera.io
  • General: hello@prospera.io

This Privacy Policy applies to Prospera Analytics (pre-launch beta) and will be updated when the platform enters general availability.
