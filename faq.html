GETTING STARTED

Prospera is a financial intelligence platform designed specifically for small and medium-sized enterprises. We take the kind of market analysis, risk assessment, and portfolio insights that institutional investors pay tens of thousands for, and make them accessible and affordable for business owners managing anywhere from $50K to $5M in investments. Whether you're a first-time founder or a seasoned entrepreneur, Prospera translates complex market data into clear, actionable insights.
Getting started takes about 5 minutes. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation, fill in your business details and email, and you'll receive a verification link. Once verified, you can connect your financial data sources and begin exploring your personalised dashboard right away. During our early access phase, you can also join the waitlist to be among the first to get access when we open new spots.
All you need is a business email address and basic information about your company. To get the most out of Prospera's analytics, you'll want to connect your business bank accounts or upload financial statements. The onboarding process guides you through each step, and our support team is available if you need any help along the way.
There is no minimum revenue requirement to use Prospera. Our platform is designed for SMEs at every stage, from early-revenue startups to established businesses. That said, our analytics are most valuable when you have at least $50K in investable capital or assets you'd like to manage more strategically. Start free with the Explorer plan, or unlock the full suite with Pro.

SECURITY & PRIVACY

Security is foundational to everything we build. We use 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest and in transit — the same standard used by major financial institutions. Your credentials are never stored on our servers; we use secure, tokenised connections through licensed data aggregators. We also design with SOC 2 principles in mind, conduct regular third-party security audits, and implement strict access controls internally. Your data is yours — we never sell or share it with third parties.
Prospera is a financial analytics and intelligence platform. We provide data visualisations, benchmarks, and market information to help you make more informed decisions — we do not provide financial advice or manage funds. We are not licensed by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) to provide financial advisory services. All investment decisions remain solely with the user. Our platform is designed to comply with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and follows GDPR-aligned data handling practices.
Yes. Prospera supports role-based access controls. As the account owner, you can invite team members and assign them specific permissions — for example, allowing your CFO full access while giving a board member view-only access to reports. You can manage these settings at any time from your account dashboard.

PLATFORM & FEATURES

Prospera provides several categories of insights tailored to your business. These include revenue and cash flow forecasting using AI models trained on your historical data and market trends; portfolio risk scoring that continuously monitors your investments; capital allocation analytics that help you visualise deployment scenarios across asset classes; industry benchmarking so you can see how your financial performance compares to peers; and real-time market alerts relevant to your specific holdings and sector.
A good financial advisor brings experience and personal judgement. Prospera complements that by providing continuous, data-driven monitoring and analysis that would be impractical for any human to do manually around the clock. Think of us as the analytical engine that runs 24/7 — tracking market movements, scoring risks, and flagging opportunities in real time. Future users will be able to work alongside advisors and use Prospera to verify data, explore alternatives, and stay informed between meetings. We're not here to replace advisors; we're here to make sure you're never flying blind.
No. Prospera is purely an analytics and intelligence platform. We provide insights, forecasts, and analysis, but we never hold, manage, or trade your funds. All investment decisions remain entirely in your hands. You maintain full control over your money at all times.
Our analysis draws from multiple sources: your connected business financial data, real-time market feeds from major global exchanges, macroeconomic indicators, industry-specific benchmarks, and alternative data signals. Our AI models synthesise these inputs to provide contextualised insights specific to your business profile, investment portfolio, and risk tolerance. All data sources are licensed and verified.

PRICING & BILLING

Prospera offers two plans. Explorer is free forever and gives you access to core portfolio tracking and basic market data. Pro is $39/month (or $349/year, saving 25%) and includes the full analytics suite — real-time data, risk scoring, benchmarking, and priority support. Both plans come with full access during a 14-day free trial. Visit our Pricing page for full details.
Yes. Every paid plan starts with a 14-day free trial that gives you full access to all Pro features — no credit card required. If you decide Prospera isn't right for you, simply let the trial expire and you'll automatically continue on the free Explorer plan with no charges or commitments.
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. We don't charge cancellation fees. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

SUPPORT & ACCOUNT

At launch, all users will have access to our help documentation and email support. Pro plan subscribers will receive priority support with faster response times and live chat access. In the meantime, you're welcome to reach out to us directly at support@prospera.io — we're a small team and we read every message.
Yes. Prospera allows you to export reports in PDF and CSV formats at any time. Your portfolio data, analytics history, and custom reports are all exportable. We believe your data belongs to you, and you should be able to take it with you whenever you need to.
