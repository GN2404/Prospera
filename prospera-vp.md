## 1. The Problem — SMEs Are Flying Blind

Singapore's SMEs account for 99% of all enterprises and employ nearly 70% of the workforce — yet the vast majority operate without access to the financial intelligence tools that drive sustainable growth.

**What SMEs are missing:**
- Real-time cash flow monitoring and runway forecasting
- Industry benchmarking against sector peers
- AI-powered risk exposure scoring
- Portfolio performance attribution
- Predictive financial modelling
- Automated alerts on financial health indicators

**Why it has been unavailable:**
- Bloomberg / Refinitiv terminals: S$2,000–4,000/month per seat
- Professional advisory services: S$500–2,000 per engagement
- Bespoke analytics platforms: Enterprise-only, 6-month onboarding
- Accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks): Backward-looking, no forecasting
- MAS licensing requirements limit advice-giving platforms

---

## 2. Our Solution — Prospera Analytics Platform

Prospera is a web-based financial analytics platform that combines real-time market data, AI-powered forecasting, and intuitive visualisations into a dashboard designed for business owners — not financial analysts.

### Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Cash Flow Intelligence** | AI forecasting predicts runway 3–12 months out. Early warnings before liquidity issues emerge. |
| **Portfolio Analytics** | Track investments and assets in a unified dashboard. Performance attribution and correlation analysis included. |
| **Risk Assessment** | Dynamic risk scoring across market, liquidity, and sector exposure. |
| **AI Insights** | Natural language summaries of financial position, trend analysis, and recommended actions — updated weekly. |
| **Sector Benchmarking** | Compare performance against Singapore industry peers on ROE, margin, and growth metrics. |
| **Smart Alerts** | Configurable alerts for cash threshold breaches, market movements, and spending anomalies. |

## 3. Target Market

**Primary segment:** Singapore-registered SMEs in the S$500K–S$20M annual revenue band — typically 5–150 employees, owner-operated or professionally managed, without a dedicated CFO or finance team.

**Secondary segment:** Founders, freelancers, and sole proprietors who manage personal and business finances together and need consolidated visibility.

**Industry focus (Phase 1):** F&B, Retail, Professional Services, Construction & Trade, Light Manufacturing.

**Market size:**
- 350,000+ SMEs in Singapore (Dept of Statistics 2024)
- ~70% of Singapore workforce employed by SMEs
- S$16B+ annual SME contribution to Singapore GDP
