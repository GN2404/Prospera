Revenue Forecasting

AI-driven models project your cash flow up to 12 months out, factoring in seasonality, historical trends, and market signals. Stop guessing — start planning with confidence. Know your runway before you need it.

Real-Time Risk Monitoring

Your risk dashboard tracks concentration risk, liquidity buffers, and market exposure in real time. Get instant alerts when thresholds are breached so you can act before small problems become big ones.

Smart Capital Allocation

Data-driven allocation analytics that help you visualise how surplus capital could be deployed across different scenarios and asset classes — against your risk tolerance, without the $25K/year price tag.

Curated feeds of SGX movements, MAS rate decisions, and sector-specific signals — filtered to what's relevant to your business. No noise, no jargon. Just the intelligence you need to make faster decisions.

Board-Ready Reports

One click generates clean, professional PDF and CSV reports formatted for directors, investors, or bankers. Customisable cover pages, commentary fields, and benchmark comparisons included at every plan level.

Built-In Compliance
