Platform Capabilities

Everything Built Into One Platform

Six core capabilities. Zero subscriptions to juggle.

1
Connect Your Accounts

Link your Singapore bank accounts and financial tools in minutes. Prospera supports DBS, OCBC, UOB, and more — with bank-grade 256-bit encryption at every step.

2
Get AI-Powered Insights

Prospera's analytics engine processes your data in real time — generating cash flow forecasts, risk scores, and sector benchmarks tailored to your exact business profile.

3
Allocate With Confidence

Use risk-scored recommendations to decide when and where to deploy surplus capital — across equities, fixed income, or growth investments — all benchmarked against your sector.

4
Grow Your Capital

Track performance against your goals and industry peers over time. Prospera learns your financial patterns and surfaces increasingly precise signals as your data grows.
