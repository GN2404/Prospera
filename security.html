Enterprise-Grade Security

Security & Trust

Your financial data deserves the highest level of protection. Here's exactly how we keep it safe.

256-bit AES Encryption PDPA Compliant No Data Selling TLS 1.3 in Transit SOC 2 Principles

256-bit AES Encryption

All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256, the same standard used by global financial institutions and government agencies. Your files, transactions, and analytics results are always encrypted on disk.

TLS 1.3 in Transit

Every connection between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.3. This ensures that data transmitted to and from the platform cannot be intercepted or read by third parties.

PDPA Compliance

We are fully compliant with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA 2012). We have appointed a Data Protection Officer and follow data minimisation principles — we only collect what is necessary.

No Data Selling — Ever

We will never sell, rent, or broker your personal or financial data to any third party. Your business data is yours. It is used solely to power the analytics you see inside your Prospera dashboard.

SOC 2 Principles

Our engineering and operational practices are designed around SOC 2 Type II principles — covering Security, Availability, and Confidentiality. We are working toward formal SOC 2 certification ahead of general availability.

Regular Security Reviews

We conduct regular internal security reviews and engage independent third-party penetration testers before each major release. Critical vulnerabilities are patched within 24 hours of discovery.

Access Controls & Infrastructure

We follow the principle of least privilege — engineers only access production systems when operationally required, and all access is logged and audited. Our infrastructure is hosted on enterprise-grade cloud providers with ISO 27001 certification.

  • Multi-factor authentication required for all internal system access
  • Separate production and development environments
  • Automated vulnerability scanning on all code deployments
  • Data backups encrypted and tested monthly

AI & Your Financial Data

Prospera uses AI models to generate financial insights. We want to be transparent:

  • Your financial data is never used to train AI models without your explicit, separate written consent
  • AI inference runs in isolated compute environments — your data is not shared with other customers' AI sessions
  • All AI-generated insights are labelled as analytical outputs, not financial advice

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability in Prospera, please report it to us responsibly at security@prospera.io. We commit to acknowledging your report within 48 hours and resolving confirmed vulnerabilities within 30 days.

We do not pursue legal action against researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith.

Questions?

  • General: hello@prospera.io
