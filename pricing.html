Yes — every Pro plan starts with a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required. If Prospera isn't for you, you'll automatically revert to the free Explorer plan.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard and keep access until the end of your billing period.
