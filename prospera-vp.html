Executive Summary

The Financial Intelligence Gap

Prospera Analytics is a Singapore-first financial intelligence platform for SMEs. We aggregate real-time market data, AI-driven cash flow forecasting, portfolio tracking, and sector benchmarking into a single, affordable dashboard.

Large enterprises spend tens of thousands of dollars per year on Bloomberg terminals, proprietary research desks, and CFO analytics teams. Prospera delivers equivalent intelligence at 1–2% of that cost — built specifically for businesses operating between S$500K and S$20M in annual revenue.

We are pre-launch, currently in closed beta, targeting General Availability in Q3 2026.

The Problem

SMEs Are Flying Blind

Singapore's SMEs account for 99% of all enterprises and employ nearly 70% of the workforce — yet the vast majority operate without access to the financial intelligence tools that drive sustainable growth. The gap is structural, not a matter of capability.

What SMEs Are Missing

  • Real-time cash flow monitoring and runway forecasting
  • Industry benchmarking against sector peers
  • AI-powered risk exposure scoring
  • Portfolio performance attribution
  • Predictive financial modelling
  • Automated alerts on financial health indicators

Why It's Been Unavailable

  • Bloomberg / Refinitiv terminals: S$2,000–4,000/mo per seat
  • Professional advisory services: S$500–2,000/engagement
  • Bespoke analytics platforms: Enterprise-only, 6-month onboarding
  • Accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks): Backward-looking, no forecasting
  • MAS licensing requirements limit advice-giving platforms

Target Market

Singapore SMEs, S$500K–S$20M Revenue

Primary segment: Singapore-registered SMEs in the S$500K–S$20M annual revenue band — typically 5–150 employees, owner-operated or professionally managed, without a dedicated CFO or finance team.

Secondary segment: Founders, freelancers, and sole proprietors who manage personal and business finances together and need consolidated visibility.

Industry focus (Phase 1): F&B, Retail, Professional Services, Construction & Trade, Light Manufacturing.
