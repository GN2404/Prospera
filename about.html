Our Story

From a simple observation to a platform built for thousands.

Early 2025

The Problem Identified

Our founder noticed that Singapore's SME owners were making critical financial decisions with incomplete data — relying on quarterly reports, outdated spreadsheets, or expensive advisors they couldn't always afford.

Mid 2025

Research & Validation

Having interviewed over 40 SME owners across sectors — F&B, logistics, retail, professional services — and confirmed a consistent gap: affordable, real-time financial intelligence simply didn't exist for businesses of their size.

Late 2025

Building Prospera

Armed with user insights, we began building — combining AI-driven analytics, Singapore market data, and a design philosophy centred on clarity. Every feature was tested with real SME owners throughout development.

2026 — Now

Launching to the World

Today, Prospera is in active development and opening its waitlist to Singapore's SME community. We're committed to launching a platform that truly earns the trust of the businesses we serve.
